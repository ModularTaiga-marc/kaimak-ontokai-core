Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

Modular Taiga SL

Barcelona, Spain

Email: privacy@modulartaiga.com

Website: https://modulartaiga.com

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Company Information: Company name, industry, location (provided during knowledge graph generation)
  • Email Address: Optional, only if you choose to create an account or subscribe to updates
  • User-Generated Content: Any data or metadata you input into OntoKai

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent (anonymized)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for session management only (no tracking/advertising cookies)

2.3 What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT collect sensitive personal data (health, financial, biometric data)
  • We do NOT track you across other websites
  • We do NOT use advertising or analytics cookies without your consent

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Service Delivery: Generate knowledge graphs, provide ontology outputs
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve OntoKai (anonymized)
  • Communication: Send service updates, respond to inquiries (only if you provided email)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: You provided explicit consent (e.g., signing up)
  • Contract: Necessary to provide the service you requested
  • Legitimate Interest: Improve our service, prevent fraud, ensure security

5. Data Sharing

5.1 We Share Data With:

  • AI Service Providers: Anthropic (Claude AI) and Groq for LLM processing
    Note: Your inputs are sent to these services to generate knowledge graphs. Review their privacy policies:
    • Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
    • Groq: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/
  • Hosting Provider: Render.com (infrastructure hosting)

5.2 We DON'T Share Data With:

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms
  • Third parties for their own marketing purposes

5.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

6. Data Retention

  • Generated Knowledge Graphs: Stored until you delete them or close your account
  • Usage Data: Anonymized and aggregated data retained for up to 2 years
  • Account Data: Retained until you request deletion

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Restriction: Request we limit processing of your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (doesn't affect prior processing)

To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@modulartaiga.com

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS: All data transmission encrypted via SSL/TLS
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data (need-to-know basis)
  • Regular Reviews: Periodic security audits and updates
  • Incident Response: Procedures to detect and respond to data breaches

Important: During beta testing, we recommend NOT inputting confidential or sensitive business data.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EU/EEA, including the United States (for AI processing via Anthropic and Groq).

We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Reliance on service providers with adequate data protection frameworks

10. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality (e.g., session management)
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do NOT use advertising or third-party tracking cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect site functionality.

11. Children's Privacy

OntoKai is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately at privacy@modulartaiga.com.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Continued use of OntoKai after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
